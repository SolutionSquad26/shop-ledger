This tool periodically saves a backup file to your device (roughly every 10 minutes while you're actively using it). On iPhone/iPad, your browser will ask "Do you want to download this file?" — always tap Download. Your license key is saved inside every backup, so if you ever switch devices or clear your browser data, just restore from your most recent backup file to get everything back, including reactivation — no need to look up your original key. You may see a few backup files with numbers after their names (e.g. "backup 2") — that's just how iPhone saves repeat downloads, and any of them will work fine to restore.
